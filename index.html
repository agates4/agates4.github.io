GoGo Drive

an iOS application using Artificial Intelligence and algorithms to create real time driving data for safer and smarter drivers

Our Inspiration

Nearly 1.3 million people die every year in car accidents and an average of 9 teens are killed every daybecause of crashes A lack of intervention and safe driving habits combine to increase the number of crashes per year  Our team created GoGoDrive - an IOS application that shows driving behavior and uses AI to detect crashes and contact local paramedics when drivers are incapacitated By tracking abrupt stops, hard accelerations, and hard swerves we provide the information parents need to improve their children’s driving and the data to help long-time drivers improve their driving behavior.  If our AI system detects a crash - the driver will be prompted to call the paramedics.  If the prompt is not disarmed after 5 minutes the local paramedics will be automatically called.(Wewill disarm because of demo purposes)
