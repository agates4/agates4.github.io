This visualization shows the relationship between the power and efficiency of cars (through MPG and horsepower) as well as their origin and manufacturer. As you can see through the graph, the American cars have the highest MPG across the board, as well as the largest horsepower (the size of the boxes). Japanese cars come second with their horsepower, and European cars come dead last in both power and efficiency.
